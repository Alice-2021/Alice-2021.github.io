Prepare

The first step to a roll tack is to prepare yourself and your boat. A lot of the times, new sailors will not have the right sail trim entering the tack as well as the right mindset. We learn from our mistakes and, in sailing, from our capsizes. Roll tacking requires you to move your body weight so that the side you are sitting on is in the water. This will cause a certain amount of fear for some because they don't want to fall in. It is better, however, to fall into the water than stay dry. When you fall in, it means you are rolling hard enough and you have the right mindset. So, going into that roll tack, keep in mind that you will never get better if you stay dry. To prepare your boat, you want to make sure that your controls are in the right place
